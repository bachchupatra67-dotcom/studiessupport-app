About StudiesSupport

StudiesSupport is a free educational platform built exclusively for ICSE and ISC students. We believe every student deserves access to clear, well-organized study materials without any distractions.

Our Mission

To make board exam preparation simple, effective, and stress-free. We provide notes written in easy English, exam-oriented questions, and revision content so students can focus on understanding rather than memorizing.

Our Team

We are a passionate team of educators and technologists committed to helping students succeed. Our team includes current and former ICSE/ISC teachers who bring their valuable classroom insights to create the best possible study materials.

What We Offer

  • Chapter-wise notes for all major subjects
  • Short and long answer questions
  • Important questions for board exams
  • Quick revision PDFs via Telegram
  • Exam tips and strategies

Our Promise

We keep things simple. No clutter, no unnecessary content — just what you need to score well. All resources are 100% free.
